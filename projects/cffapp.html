Campus Friends Forever (CFF)

From DIG3811: User-Centered Design

Project Overview

This project is a mobile app prototype called Campus Friends Forever (CFF) for DIG3811, a User-Centered Design course. The application is designed to facilitate social connections among university students, allowing them to find and connect with friends on campus.
